Welcome to the Zenflow help page!

Here you'll find information and resources to help you get the most out of the Zenflow app.

What is Zenflow?

Zenflow is a task management app designed to help you and your team stay organized and on track. With Zenflow, you can create tasks, assign them to team members, track progress, and collaborate with others in real-time.

Getting Started

To get started with Zenflow, simply sign up for an account and log in. Once you're logged in, you can create a new project, add team members, and start creating tasks.

Creating Tasks

To create a new task, click the "New Task" button on the workflow page. From there, you can enter the task details, such as the task name, description, and due date.

Tracking Progress

To track the progress of a task, simply view the task page. From there, you can see the task status, comments, attachments, and any updates that have been made.

Collaborating with Others

To collaborate with others on a task, simply leave a comment on the task page. You can also attach links to the task to share with others.

Support

If you have any questions or issues with Zenflow, please contact our support team at brandon.grossman@ndsu.edu. We're always here to help!
